A little bit about me

I'm Scott Mangiapane, a software engineer in Chicago, IL. I write a lot of code, but I have other hobbies as well... Swimming, tennis, piano, chess, and leathercraft to name a few. Most recently I've been learning the bass guitar.

My engineering journey

I started programming on my TI-84 graphing calculator back in high school. I didn't have a smartphone at the time, so I made my own versions of popular phone games on my calculator and distributed them to my classmates (to the dismay of my math teachers). I've come a long way since then, but have maintained that same enthusiasm.

I've spent the last ____ as a full-stack engineer at GoDaddy building the API and dashboard for their VPS (virtual private server) product. Most recently, I've learned Go and have been studying AWS.
